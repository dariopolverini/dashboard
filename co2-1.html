Reducing Emissions with Natural Solutions

Traditional Mechanical Treatment

  • Uses energy for aeration, pumping, and mechanical processes.
  • Emits approximately 1 kg CO₂/m³ (+/- 20%).
  • For 1440 m³/year of wastewater (the average wastewater generated in the innovation hub in one year), emissions would be 1.44 ton CO₂e/year.

Constructed Wetlands: A Low-Impact Alternative

  • Works through natural filtration, sedimentation, and biological processes.
  • Requires little to no energy input, drastically cutting emissions.
  • Emits only 0.05 kg CO₂e/m³ (+/- 20%).
  • For 1440 m³/year, emissions are just 0.07 ton CO₂e/year.

Result: 95% Emission Reduction

By implementing Constructed Wetlands, the Innovation Hub achieves a near-total reduction in emissions, making wastewater treatment more sustainable and eco-friendly.
