SUSTAINABLE CONSTRUCTION SITE SOLUTIONS

At Bauer Resources Saudi and Energia, we are committed to creating the most sustainable construction site possible. We focus on reducing both direct and indirect CO2 emissions while promoting circularity throughout the site. Here's how we are making a difference:

1. Solar-Powered Electricity

Our site runs entirely on solar energy, providing all the electricity needed for construction activities. With this clean energy solution, we have ZERO emissions, ensuring that no direct CO2 is produced during site operations. This helps us minimize our environmental footprint and contribute to a cleaner future.

2. Eco-Friendly Wastewater Treatment with Reedbox

We utilize Reedbox®, our proprietary movable solution, to treat all sewage generated on-site. This constructed wetland system is specifically designed for remote locations, offering an eco-friendly way to handle wastewater. The treated water is then reused for irrigating our nursery, where we grow plants for the Al Bada Constructed Wetland Project.

3. Organic Waste Composting with Bokashi System

To reduce waste and create valuable resources, we compost all food leftovers and organic waste from the site using the Bokashi system. The result is high-quality fertilizer and compost, which we use to support the growth of plants in our nursery.

Promoting Circularity and Reducing Indirect Emissions

By implementing these sustainable practices, we reduce unnecessary material and water deliveries to the site, thus lowering indirect emissions. Our approach is not just about waste reduction—it’s about creating a fully circular system that supports both the environment and the local community.
