Local Resources for a Greener Solution

Utilizing Locally Sourced Materials

Constructed wetlands can be built entirely with materials sourced within Saudi Arabia, eliminating the need for long-distance transportation and reducing hidden emissions. By using locally available resources, this approach contributes to a more sustainable and environmentally responsible treatment solution.

Sustainable Filtration Media

High-quality basalt gravel from Jabal Al-Barak in Tabuk is used to support wastewater treatment by providing an ideal medium for biofilm growth.

Locally Manufactured Components

Waterproofing: Achieved with an HDPE liner made in Dammam.

Piping System: uPVC pipes and accessories (elbows, tees, reducers, and couplings) produced in Jubail.

Denitrification Tank: Made from GRP and sourced from Dammam.

Circularity Through Recycled Materials

The denitrification tank utilizes recycled bottle caps, repurposing waste into a functional treatment component. These bottle caps are corrosion-resistant and provide optimal microbial growth surfaces, enhancing biological treatment efficiency.
