The Innovation Hub Constructed Wetland is a pioneering project developed by NEOM’s Engineering Innovation (EI) Department at the Design and Construction Sector. Designed to showcase sustainable water treatment technology, this demonstration wetland highlights the potential of nature-based solutions for efficient and eco-friendly wastewater management.

A Holistic Approach to Sustainability

This project integrates green technologies to minimize carbon footprint while ensuring effective water reuse. Key features include:

  • Passive Treatment System: The constructed wetland treats sewage water from the Innovation Hub’s toilet facilities without requiring external energy, achieving NCEC 2021 irrigation standards.
  • Water Reuse & Conservation: Treated water irrigates the surrounding landscape, while excess water maintains freshwater levels in the nearby recreational pond.

By combining innovation with ecological design, the Innovation Hub Constructed Wetland exemplifies NEOM’s commitment to sustainable development and circular water management.
